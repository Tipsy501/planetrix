About Planetrix Business Consultants

Planetrix Business Consultants is a Gaborone-based firm specializing in business consulting, tendering services, and administrative support. We help organizations identify opportunities and implement effective solutions that lead to sustainable growth.

Our deep understanding of the local business landscape in Botswana allows us to provide insights that are both practical and impactful. Whether you're a startup looking to register or an established firm seeking to win major tenders, we are here to support your journey.
